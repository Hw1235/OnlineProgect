1. **_Exponentiation_**

Two asterisks (\*\*) are used to raise a number to the power of another, which is called exponentiation.

For example:

```js
let x = 2;
let y = 5;
console.log(x ** y);
```

_Fill in the blank to output 5 raised to the 3rd power._

```js
console.log(5 ** 3);
```
